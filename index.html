    Our Vision & Mission

    #StandWithUkraine is non-profit Canadian organization supporting the people of Ukraine in their pursuit of a democratic society with dignity, justice, and human and civil rights. All fundraising money will directly go to the National Bank of Ukraine (NBU) Special Account to Raise Funds for Ukraine’s Armed Forces & women with children affected by the war. It's a terrifying time for Ukraine and our people. We are in the middle of unimaginable aggression and fighting for our freedom as hard as we can!
